About Us

Established in 2007, S.E.A College of Engineering and Technology, Bangalore, is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by AICTE. We offer a range of programs including B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA, with a focus on academic excellence and industry readiness.

Our mission is to nurture globally competitive professionals through innovation and quality education.
