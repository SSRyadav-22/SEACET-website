Admissions 2025

Admissions to B.Tech programs are through KCET or COMEDK UGET. Minimum eligibility: 45% in 12th (PCM).

Entrance Exam Cutoffs (Approximate)

  • KCET: 50,000 - 65,000 rank for CSE, AIML, IoT
  • KCET: 60,000 - 80,000 rank for Mech, ECE, ISE
  • COMEDK: 40,000 - 60,000 rank for all branches

Apply online at www.seaedu.ac.in or visit the campus.
